Mountain Retreat Kodai

Lake View

Mountain Retreat Kodai is a 15 room, luxury boutique resort nestled in the hills overlooking the famous lake of Kodaikanal, a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu, India. This retreat boasts several scenic and unpopulated views of this lake from different parts of the property. A contemporary mountain building constructed with locally quarried stone; it is surrounded by a multi-level garden space allow you to experience the outdoors from within.

Modern Residence

Property

The resort is situated on a sprawling property surrounded by dense forests and offers panoramic views of the valley below. The architecture of the resort is inspired by the colonial era and yet exudes a contemporary vibe. The property is tastefully designed, featuring woodden flooring, and floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the lake or the hills, offering guests their own private memories of a destination well chosen.

Rooms

Fine Dining

Dining

The dining area also offers some wonderful views, with our signature floor-to-ceiling windows. The kitchen garden includes a herb patch and a salad garden. Scenic Tours

Travel

Kodai is full of unique experiences, which you can partake of at leisure. Spend some time in nature, walking through waterfalls, and hiking through the forests. Soak in the birdsong; look up at the clouds; stargaze at night. Our travel desk can organize tours to popular nearby sites such as Poombarai, Vattakanal, and Mannavanur, with its sheep farm and tranquil lake. Golfing can be arranged at the local golf course (4 km away) by prior appointment. From full-day excursions to nearby explorations, we can organize tailor-made itineraries to suit each traveller through our network of experienced guides.
